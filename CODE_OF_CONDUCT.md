# Code of Conduct

SliceFx aims to be a small, welcoming technical project.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Keep feedback focused on code, design, documentation, and project goals.
- Help keep discussions accessible to newcomers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, block participants, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community. If you need to report a conduct issue, contact the maintainers through the repository's private channels where available.
